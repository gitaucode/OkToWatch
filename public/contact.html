Common questions
How does beta access work? ▾
OkToWatch is currently free during beta. Just create an account to unlock saved lists, child profiles, and synced history. We’ll give advance notice before any paid plans return.
Do I need to pay during beta? ▾
No. Beta access is free and does not require a credit card.
The analysis for a title seems wrong — what do I do? ▾
Use the "Report an issue" button above and tell us the title and what you think is incorrect. We review all reports and update our analysis where needed.
How do I delete my account? ▾
You can delete your account instantly from Settings → Danger Zone → Delete account. This removes all your data from our servers immediately.
Do you have an app? ▾
OkToWatch is a Progressive Web App (PWA) — you can install it on your home screen from any browser. On iPhone, tap the Share button then "Add to Home Screen". On Android, tap the menu then "Install app".
