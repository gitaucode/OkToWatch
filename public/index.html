Transparency

Who reviews these titles?

Parents are right to be skeptical of algorithms. Every rating combines reviewer input, child-development guidance, and human oversight.

50K+ titles reviewed across movies, shows, games, and apps
10 sec average time from search to decision signal
12K+ verified parent reviewers contributing notes and flags
4 bands age-aware safety thresholds for real childhood stages
01

Verified parent reviewers

Over 12,000 active parents flag specific content moments — not just vibes. Their notes appear directly in your breakdown so you see exactly what they saw.

02

Child psychology guidance

Our age-band criteria are shaped by child psychologists and educators — so ratings reflect emotional readiness, not just content categories from a rating board.

03

Editorial human oversight

Every title goes through an editorial review before publishing. No automated rating goes live without a human check. Ever.
