What we collect

  • check_circle Account info: Email, name, and password (hashed)
  • check_circle Search history: Titles you search — only stored while you're logged in and with your consent
  • check_circle Watchlist: Movies/shows you save to your lists
  • check_circle Usage analytics: Which pages you visit (anonymous, no tracking)

Why we collect it

We collect minimal data to provide OkToWatch:

  • ✓ Your account info lets you log in and save preferences
  • ✓ Search history helps us improve recommendations and the app
  • ✓ Watchlist data syncs across your devices
  • ✓ Analytics help us understand which features matter to parents

How we protect it

  • check_circle SSL/TLS encryption: All data in transit is encrypted
  • check_circle Database encryption: Passwords are hashed with industry-standard algorithms
  • check_circle Limited access: Only essential staff can access your data
  • check_circle Regular audits: We audit our security practices regularly

What we don't do

  • close Sell data: We never sell your info to third parties
  • close Track you across sites: We don't use tracking pixels or cross-site cookies
  • close Share with advertisers: Your data never reaches advertisers
  • close Use for AI training: We don't train models on user data without consent

Your rights

You own your data. You have the right to:

  • ✓ Request a copy of all your data (GDPR, CCPA compliant)
  • ✓ Update or delete your account anytime from your settings
  • ✓ Opt out of non-essential analytics
  • ✓ Export your watchlist anytime

Questions?

We believe privacy should be simple and transparent. If anything here is unclear or you have concerns, please reach out.
