Verdict System

What the verdicts actually mean

Every breakdown is built to answer the real parent question: is this okay for my child right now? The verdict gives you a fast summary, and the category details explain why.

check_circle Approved

A strong fit for the selected age or profile, with content levels that stay within the filters you chose.

schedule Caution

Not a clear no, but worth a closer look. These titles may include some mature moments that depend on your child and your family rules.

block Blocked

The title crosses one or more filters you set, such as age limits, strong language, graphic violence, or other restricted content.

info Details Matter

The verdict is the shortcut. The full page explains which categories drove the result so you can make the final call with context.

Common Tasks

The most important things you can do in OkToWatch

Search and read a title breakdown

Find a movie or show, then scan the verdict and category notes.

expand_more
Search by title from the homepage, search modal, or search page. If we find a strong match, we take you directly to the detailed result page.
On the result page, read the top verdict first, then scan the categories that matter most to your household.

Use profiles to tailor the app for each child

Profiles make verdicts and recommendations more useful for real families.

expand_more
Create one profile per child with their age and content limits. OkToWatch can then adjust guidance, lists, and filters around that profile.
If one child can handle more than another, you do not need to remember separate rules in your head every time you search.

Save lists and check history later

Keep track of what has already been reviewed and what is still under consideration.

expand_more
Add approved titles to family-safe lists, block titles you do not want resurfacing, and use history to remember past decisions.
This is especially helpful when another parent, grandparent, or sitter asks about a title you already checked.

Share a result without sharing your whole account

Useful for co-parents, grandparents, babysitters, and school-night backup plans.

expand_more
Open a title page and use the share action to send the verdict and reasoning to someone else who needs the same context.
That way you are not forwarding screenshots or rewriting the same explanation every time someone asks if a title is okay.
