Manage your family's viewing experience. Create a profile for each child so content guidance, lists, and filters match their age and your household rules.

Parent Tip

Set each child up once, then let the app do the sorting.

When each child has their own profile, OkToWatch can tailor search results, recommendations, and saved lists to the right age and restrictions automatically. It keeps movie night simpler and reduces second-guessing every time you check a new title.
